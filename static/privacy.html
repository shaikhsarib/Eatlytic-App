Data Minimization

We do not ask for your email, phone number, or social profiles to scan. We use a cryptographically signed Device ID stored in a secure cookie to track your free scan quota.

Scan Privacy

Photos you upload are processed for OCR text extraction. We store the nutritional data extracted to improve our model, but we do not link these images to your real-world identity.

Right to Erasure

You can clear your local history and request data deletion at any time. Our servers automatically purge unlinked scan data every 90 days.

DPDP Compliance

We comply with the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act. Your health data is processed solely for the purpose of providing nutritional insights.
